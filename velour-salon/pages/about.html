12 Years of Art

The Beginning

A Vision for Luxury in Every Detail

Founded in 2013 by beauty entrepreneur Ria Khanna, Velour Salon was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel extraordinary. Starting as a boutique studio in Meerut, we've grown into one of the region's most sought-after luxury beauty destinations.

Our philosophy combines international techniques with deeply personal service — creating transformations that go far beyond the mirror.

Our Journey

A Timeline of Excellence

2013

Velour is Founded

Ria Khanna opens a 3-chair boutique studio with a dream of redefining luxury beauty in Meerut.

2016

First Expansion

Studio doubles in size. Bridal and special occasion packages introduced. Team grows to 10 stylists.

2018

Spa Wing Opens

Velour launches its signature spa and skin treatment wing, bringing holistic wellness to clients.

2021

Award Recognition

Named "Best Luxury Salon in Western UP" — a testament to our team's dedication and excellence.

2025

Digital Rebrand

New premium experience launches with online booking, digital consultations, and a membership program.
